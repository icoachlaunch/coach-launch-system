# The Bamboo

- **Placement:** Pre-Pitch
- **SCORE™ act / phase:** Opportunity · 2 WHAT
- **Job:** hype / empowering-belief
- **Target (the belief/objection it breaks):** "It's not working fast enough, so it must not be working." (They quit right before the payoff because early effort is invisible.)
- **Soundbyte:** "The growth was happening the whole time — underground."
- **Source:** Adapted from the widely-told Chinese bamboo tree parable (common public analogy). Told without exact year/height figures to avoid overstating.
- **Runtime:** ~2–3 min

---

## 1 · HOOK
"How many of you have ever started something — eating right, building a business,
learning a skill — done everything right for a while, saw nothing, and wanted to quit?
Type **yep**."

## 2 · STORY
"There's a plant called the Chinese bamboo tree. And it does something strange.

You plant the seed. You water it. You give it sun. And for a long time… nothing. No
sprout. No shoot. Nothing you can see.

A year goes by. Still nothing. You'd swear the seed was dead. Most people would dig it
up and give up.

But the ones who keep watering — who trust it — one day the bamboo breaks the surface.
And then it doesn't crawl. It **shoots** up, tall, fast, further than you'd believe a
plant could grow in a season.

So here's the question. Did that bamboo grow in those few weeks? Type your answer.

No. It was growing the entire time. Down. Building a root system deep and strong enough
to hold everything that was coming. You just couldn't see it."

## 3 · TURN
"How many of you are in your underground season right now? You're putting in the work
and the surface still looks like dirt.

That's not failure. That's roots. The people who make it aren't the ones who grow
fastest — they're the ones who keep watering when they can't see a thing yet."

## 4 · TARGET
Reframes slow early progress as necessary root-building, not proof of failure. It
inoculates against the "I've tried before and nothing happened" quit-reflex and builds
belief that the system pays off if they stay in — which sets up committing to the offer.

## 5 · SOUNDBYTE
"The growth was happening the whole time — underground."

## 6 · TRANSITION
"So don't dig up your bamboo. Let me show you the roots we're going to build today —
and what breaks the surface when you do."

---

### Placement notes
Great in the Opportunity act to create belief in the system's payoff, or as a
mid-event re-energizer. Pairs naturally with a Heaven-with-the-system beat.
