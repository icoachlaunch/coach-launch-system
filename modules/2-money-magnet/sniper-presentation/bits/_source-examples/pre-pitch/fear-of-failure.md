# Fear of Failure

- **Placement:** Pre-Pitch
- **SCORE™ act / phase:** Serendipity+Connection · 1 WHO
- **Job:** empowering-belief
- **Target (the belief/objection it breaks):** "Fear of failure is keeping me from taking the shot."
- **Soundbyte:** "You have to fail to win."
- **Source:** Repurposed from your example (raw: `_source-examples/_originals/pre-pitch/Fear Of Failure BITS.docx`). Credits Steve Harvey; uses public examples (Buffett, Jordan, batting averages).
- **Runtime:** ~3 min
- **Structure used:** Story Jam — I · Point · They · Soundbyte

---

## 1 · HOOK
"How many of you have held back from something because you were scared it wouldn't work?
Type **me.**"

## 2 · STORY
"Failure is part of the process. Steve Harvey — great speaker and comedian — taught me
that you have to fail to win. You have to lose your fear of failure. People who never
fail never try.

You gotta get it wrong to get it right. You learn nothing from winning — you only learn
from your failures.

Warren Buffett said he wouldn't invest in someone's business unless they'd failed in two
other businesses first — because now they know what NOT to do.

Michael Jordan took 949 game-winning shots. He only made 123 of them. The greatest
basketball player who ever lived — and he *took* 949. All you have to do is win some of
the time.

A pro baseball player who hits the ball 3 times out of 10 makes millions. A basketball
player who makes half his shots makes millions. You don't have to be perfect."

## 3 · TURN
"**Point:** all you need to do is learn to take the shot — and keep taking shots until
you make it.

**They:** the voice inside your head will tell you — *you can't do this, you're not cut
out to be an entrepreneur, what will people think of you if you fail?* That voice is the
thing to beat, not the failure."

## 4 · TARGET
Kills fear-of-failure paralysis early so "what if it doesn't work for me?" is already
reframed before the offer. Failure becomes evidence you're trying, not a reason to stop.

## 5 · SOUNDBYTE
"You have to fail to win." (Closer: *"Fear is the strangling cloak of self-sabotage."*)

## 6 · TRANSITION
"So today you take the shot. Type **taking the shot.** Let me show you where to aim."
